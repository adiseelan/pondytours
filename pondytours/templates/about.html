About Pondy Weekend Tours

I am an MBA Tourism graduate based in Pondicherry, passionate about creating authentic and memorable local travel experiences.

Pondy Weekend Tours was started with a simple idea — to provide safe, affordable and well-planned weekend tours for couples, families and solo travelers.

As a local guide, I ensure:

  • ✔ Personalized tour experience
  • ✔ Trusted local knowledge
  • ✔ Safe and comfortable travel
  • ✔ Transparent pricing

Why Choose Us?

Unlike big tour operators, we focus on small group experiences, giving you the real feel of Pondicherry — beaches, heritage streets, Auroville visits, and hidden local gems.
